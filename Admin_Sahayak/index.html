About Sahayak

Sahayak is a system that is easy to deploy and efficient for administration that is managing a disaster. The system will allow the admins and the end user to have a efficient and easy way to access the most important necessities during the time of the disaster.Making high quality information management systems for emergency preparedness, response, recovery and resilience-building accessible to all.
